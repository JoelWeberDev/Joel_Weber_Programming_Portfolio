Passions

My Christian Idenity

Perhaps the most defining aspect about me is that I am follower of Jesus Christ because He willingly endured the consequence for my failures and mistakes. For this reason, I seek to apply and integrate Jesus's loving and perfect lifestyle into my demeanor and practices. I am amazed as I observe the profound impact that following Jesus has in the lives of others. It inspires me to consider how much accomplished when people in through a common love choose to forgo their differences and work together with a united purpose.

Inspiring Others to Achieve

I believe that there is incredible value when people are inspired to achieve and excel as they go through life. Motivation, however, is difficult to synthesize independently, and is often generated through interpersonal interactions with people who listen and offer insights. Often, I have been the beneficiary of people who are willing to invest their time to help inspire others. So, now I too want to motivate others achieve their goals and transform their dreams into reality.

Expanding Knowledge

I regard every aspect of life is an opportunity to learn something new regardless of how menial or insignificant it may seem. Hence, I look to use every task to expand my general competence by maintaining mental engagement in whatever I do. Even while I am working full time, I devote a portion of my spare time to completing online courses primarily in computer science, mathematics, and science.

Finally, an old cliche saying states “If you're the smartest person in the room, you are in the wrong room.” As I converse with others this saying challenges me to identify the expertise of others and direct the conversation towards those proficiencies rather than always revolving around my own. Hence, I effectively become the less competent individual in the room with great potential to learn something new.
