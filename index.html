Recent Projects

I love ideas, whether simple or complex, outrageous or grounded I will gravitate towards them. However, what I enjoy still more than a good idea is to bring a thought to life through building them. Some particular engineering sandboxes where I play include, agricultural technology, robotics, computer vision, physics, mathematical modelling and web development to name a few. When it comes to engineering I am not only afraid to try, but I also value the grit required to see a project through until absolute failure or success.

More About Me

I grew up in rural Ontario near Listowel where I attended high school and nurtured a desire to develop automated solutions to drive efficiency through both web and robotics applications. When I am not engineering, I love to play sports, go back country camping, concoct quirky gadgets, listen to podcasts, run, and read science, philosophy, or classics.

The Resume

My profession of engineering in computer science and physics is founded upon much hands on experience and a strong practical basis. Throughout highschool I worked long hours as farm hand. Then I worked as a technician installing high pressure air systems on farm equipment. After high school I chose to spend a year working as an HVAC technician before university. From this experience I have seen the value that simple, commonsense engineering can bring to the consumer and the technicians servicing the products that I build.
